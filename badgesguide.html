How to get them all!

How to get the badges

Speedrunner

"Explode the reactor within 8 minutes"

  1. Disable Auto Scram
  2. Do this by disabling Recirc Pumps and isolation valves and withdrawing rods as fast as possible.
  3. Explode the reactor within 8 minutes.

?????

"What?"

  1. Climb on top of the first cooling tower, the left one relative to the train tracks.
  2. Turn left and walk around the cooling tower until you see a floppa cube.
  3. Touch the floppa.

#1 Enemy of staff morale

"Throw all the pizza into the spent fuel pool."

  1. Grab all the pizza from the staff cafeteria. You can do so by repeatably touching the pizza circle.
  2. Head to the Reactor Hall.
  3. When entering, there will be a water pool to your right.
  4. Drop the pizzas in your inventory into the pool by pressing backspace.

Deep Fried

"Make contact with live wires in the bridge circuit room."

  1. Go to either one of the generator bridge circuit rooms located on the left and right side of the Turbine Sub Level.
  2. Go behind the breakers, away from the barrier, then head to the middle of the breakers.
  3. Jump up and down until you get electricuted. Try to hit the wires.

Sufficiently ionized

"Reach a fatal dosage of ionizing radiation."

  1. Go to a radiation source. Best places are the spent fuel pools and reactor hall.
  2. Stay there until your dosimeter reads 500 rem.

Power to the people

"Connect to the grid and deliver power."

  1. Successfully sync a turbine and start producing positive power.

BOOP!

":3c"

  1. Go to the turbine hall entrance located next to the control room.
  2. Take the south stairwell and climb to the top.
  3. Turn left and go through the door.
  4. Walk forward until you see a blue furry plushy. Click on it.

Floor pizza

"Kinda gross, ngl"

  1. While at the elevators in the entrance of the facility, head to level 2.
  2. Take the door on the far left.
  3. Look to your left until you see a pizza with a wet sign.
  4. Touch the loose pizza slice and eat it.

You're fired!

"Fail to produce any power within 48 hours"

  1. Don't make any power for 2 days.

7 Nights at Oakridge

"Complete a whole week at the plant."

  1. Successfully produce power and maintain the reactor for 7 days without meltdowns.

Senior Operator

"You've proven yourself in the art of reactor operation."

  1. Gain 500,000 total points as an operator.

By the book

"Reach 100% efficiency while meeting demand."

  1. You can reach 100% efficiency by lowering the amount of bypass being used by the turbine while meeting demand.
  2. Lower reactor power in 50 MW increments until the bypass on the turbine is fully closed. (This requires using Auto Bypass)

Teamwork? Sounds like a liability

"Complete an entire shift all on your own."

  1. Complete a shift by yourself without exploding/melting the reactor.

GET ME FEEDWATER!

"Don't always take things at face value."

  1. To trigger this easter egg, toggle the red/grey switches located on the end of the operator panel.
  2. When the reactor water level line graph shows a straight line, click on it multiple times.

Junior operator

"Hope you were paying attention."

  1. Reach 75,000 total points as a operator.

Energy tycoon

"Sell over 20 gigawatt-hours of power in one day"

  1. Sell a total of 20000 MW of power in a single day.
  2. You can see the amount of power sold by looking straight up above the condensate pump panel.

Supervisor

"Share your wisdom and guide your peers to success!"

  1. Earn a total of 1,250,000 points as an operator

OSHA would be proud

"Safely shut down the reactor without mishap."

  1. Reach the end of a shift and receive the call to shutdown the reactor.
  2. Follow the shutdown checklist.
  3. Ensure that the following systems are enabled/disabled before requesting inspection:
    • Turbine Gear is engaged for both turbines.
    • Breaker 18M-G1 is off.
    • Offline Core Cooling and OCC pumps are enabled.
  4. You should receive this badge if you meet all the criteria for a good shutdown.

Shift Manager

"Middle management, maximum ego."

  1. Reach a total of 2,500,000 points as an operator.
