Emergency Procedure

Many situations can lead to an emergency procedure. Use the quick links below to navigate to each scenario:

Abnormal RPV Pressure Abnormal RPV Temperature Abnormal Water Level Fuel Channel Leak Loss of Offsite Power (LOOP)

Abnormal RPV Pressure

Description: The pressure inside the RPV (Reactor Pressure Vessel) is above safe limits.

IF High DO
RELIEF VALVES AS RQRD
CONTROL RODS INSERT
TURBINE BYPASS VALVE AS RQRD
RPV Pressure MONITOR
IF High High DO
AZ-5 PRESS
ALL RELIEF VALVES OPEN
TURBINE BYPASS VALVE 100%
RPV Pressure MONITOR

Abnormal RPV Temperature

Description: The temperature inside the RPV (Reactor Pressure Vessel) is above safe limits.

IF High DO
MAIN CIRCULATION PUMPS AS RQRD
CONTROL RODS INSERT
RPV Temperature MONITOR
IF High High DO
AZ-5 PRESS
MAIN CIRCULATION PUMPS A1 & A2 ENABLE
MAIN CIRCULATION PUMPS B1, B2, C1 & C2 DISABLE
MAIN CIRCULATION PUMPS A1 & A2 SPEED 3
RELIEF VALVES AS RQRD
RPV Temperature MONITOR

Abnormal Water Level

Description: Either high or low water level in the primary coolant circuit.

IF High DO
FEEDWATER PUMPS AS RQRD - DISABLE
CIRC. DRAIN 1 ENABLE
CIRC. DRAIN 2 AS RQRD
Water Level MONITOR
IF Low DO
Reactor Power HOLD
FEEDWATER PUMPS AS RQRD - INCREASE
Water Level MONITOR
ECCS READY
IF Low Low DO
AZ-5 PRESS
ECCS INJECT
FEEDWATER PUMPS AS RQRD - INCREASE
Water Level MONITOR

Fuel Channel Leak

Description: Fuel channel ruptured. May cause power surges and water level drop beyond feedwater recovery capability.

IF Fuel Channel Leak DO
AZ-5 PRESS
ALL RELIEF VALVES OPEN
OCC PUMPS 1 & 2 ENABLE
HEAT EXCHANGER A & B ENABLE
IF SHUTDOWN SUCCESSFUL DO
ENGINEERING CALL - REPAIR DAMAGED SYSTEMS
IF SHUTDOWN UNSUCCESSFUL DO
EVACUATION BEGIN

Loss of Offsite Power (LOOP)

Description: Offsite power grid has failed. All systems run on onsite diesel generators, they don't produce enough power to keep reactor online. Core Cooling is critical.

IF LOOP DO
AZ-5 PRESS
BREAKER 64A-M DISABLE
BREAKER 64A-G2 DISABLE
EMER. DIESEL GEN. 1,2 START
EMER. DIESEL GEN. 1,2 AWAIT - AT SPEED
EMER. DIESEL GEN. 1,2 BREAKER ENABLE
BREAKER 480LV-A VERIFY ENABLED
ENGINEERING CALL - RESET BREAKERS
OFFLINE CORE COOLING SYSTEMS VERIFY WORKING
TEMPERATURE, PRESSURE, WATER LEVEL MONITOR
