What are Rod Control Patterns?



Rod Control Patterns are a number of procedures introduced by the community to guide you through a safe reactor startup or change of power during operations. The RBMK-1500 features a total of 97 control rods consisting of 16 shutdown control rods, 17 automatic control rods, and the remaining 64 are manually control by you. Rods in the center of the core operate at higher power and temperature, while rods near the edges run cooler with lower power, keep this in mind in case you do not want to follow any of the patterns further below.


How to select rods?



There are 97 LED lamps in total, each is positioned above every rod control button. They indicate if the rod is selected by glowing a bright yellow color, or if the rod is unselected by not glowing. To select a rod click on it's respective button, the LED lamp above it will light up and indicate the rod is selected.You will not be able to select automatic or shutdown control rods manually.
